How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Vaulty account and all associated data directly from within the app. Follow these steps:

Step 1

Open the Vaulty app on your device

Step 2

Go to Settings tab at the bottom

Step 3

Tap Account

Step 4

Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"

Step 5

Confirm deletion — your account and all data will be permanently deleted

What Gets Deleted

  • ✅ Your account credentials
  • ✅ All your documents and expiry data
  • ✅ All cloud backup data
  • ✅ All personal information
