Our Commitment

Vaulty ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Vaulty mobile application.

Vaulty is designed with privacy in mind.

  • Personal Information: Vaulty does not require users to provide personal information such as name, email address, or phone number unless explicitly required for specific functionality.

Any information collected is used solely to:

Data Storage

Notifications

Vaulty may send notifications related to reminders and alerts configured within the application. These notifications are generated based on user-provided data and are fully controllable within the app settings.

Children's Privacy

Vaulty is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
